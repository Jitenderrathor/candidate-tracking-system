User Manual: Candidate Tracking System
Welcome to your Candidate Tracking System! This software is designed to make recruiting simple, organized, and efficient. Think of it as a smart, digital filing cabinet for all the people applying for jobs at your company.

This guide will explain how everything works in plain, easy-to-understand language.

1. The Dashboards (Your Command Centers)
   When you open the application, you'll see a dashboard. This is your command center that gives you a quick overview of what's happening.

Public Dashboard
What it is: A view that anyone can see, even without logging in.
What it does: It shows high-level statistics, such as how many people have registered, where they are coming from (like LinkedIn or Facebook), and how many people have been selected or rejected.
Why it's useful: It's great for sharing general progress with the wider team or management without giving them full access to sensitive candidate data.
Internal Dashboard
What it is: Your private, logged-in view.
What it does: It shows similar statistics but includes a Quick Actions menu. From here, you can quickly jump to adding a new candidate, viewing reports, importing Excel files, or managing the Recycle Bin.
Why it's useful: It serves as the starting point for your daily work. 2. Managing Candidates (The Core Feature)
The Candidates page is where you will spend most of your time. This is your master list of everyone who has applied.

Viewing: You can see a table of all candidates. You can search for a specific person by name, phone number, or Candidate ID.
Adding a Candidate: Click the "Add Candidate" button to manually fill out a form for a new applicant. You can enter their details like name, email, skills, and even paste a link to their resume (Drive Link).
Updating Status: As a candidate moves through the hiring process, you can update their status (e.g., from "Registered" to "Under Consideration" to "Selected").
Bulk Actions: If you want to delete multiple people at once, you can use the checkboxes next to their names to select them, and then click "Delete Selected" to remove them in one go. 3. Excel Import (Bulk Upload)
If you have a spreadsheet full of candidates, you don't need to type them in one by one!

How it works: Go to the Excel Import page. You can drag and drop your .xlsx or .csv file here.
Smart Matching: The system is smart! If your spreadsheet has a column named "File URL" or "Resume URL", the system will automatically turn that into a clickable "Download Resume" link for that candidate.
Error Checking: If there are mistakes in your spreadsheet (like a missing email address or an invalid phone number), the system will tell you exactly which row has the problem so you can fix it before importing. 4. Recycle Bin (Your Safety Net)
Accidentally deleted a candidate? Don't worry!

How it works: When you delete a candidate, they aren't gone forever. They are moved to the Recycle Bin.
Restoring Data: You can visit the Recycle Bin from the side menu, find the deleted person, and click Restore. They will instantly be moved back to your active Candidates list.
Automatic Cleanup: To save space, any candidate left in the Recycle Bin for 30 days will be permanently and automatically deleted.
Wipe Complete Data: If you are an Admin and want to start fresh, you can use the "Wipe Complete Data" button on the dashboard. This moves everyone to the Recycle Bin, effectively resetting your system back to zero. 5. Reports & Analytics
The Reports page helps you understand your recruitment performance over time.

What it shows: You can filter the data by date, status, or source. It will show you exactly how many candidates fall into those categories.
Why it's useful: If you want to know how many people from Instagram were hired last month, you can easily find out here. 6. User Management (Controlling Access)
Depending on your role (System Administrator, HR Manager, or Recruiter), you have different permissions.

System Administrators: Can see and do everything. They can manage other users (change passwords, add new recruiters) and access the Recycle Bin.
HR Managers & Recruiters: Can manage candidates and view reports, but cannot access system settings like user management or the Recycle Bin.
You can manage all of these accounts from the Users page on the sidebar.

Need to Change Your Password?
If you ever need to update your password, simply click on your initial in the top right corner of the screen (your Profile Menu) and select Change Password.

If you have any questions or run into issues while using the application, always feel free to ask for assistance!
